Pearl and SOTA have partnered to offer native support for Pearl\u2019s industry-leading, FDA-cleared radiologic AI solution Second Opinion\u00ae within the SOTA Cloud software platform. SOTA Cloud will be the first dental imaging software in the United States to offer a fully-integrated clinical AI featureset.<\/em><\/div>\n
\n
\n

 <\/p>\n

\u200dLOS ANGELES, California — October 18, 2022<\/strong>\u00a0–\u00a0Pearl<\/a>, the global leader in dental AI solutions, and\u00a0SOTA Imaging<\/a>, the leading manufacturer of dental imaging hardware and software, announced today a partnership to integrate Pearl\u2019s FDA-cleared\u00a0Second Opinion\u00ae<\/a>\u00a0real-time clinical pathology detection artificial intelligence (AI) solution with SOTA\u2019s popular\u00a0SOTA Cloud<\/a>\u00ae dental imaging software. Native support for Pearl\u2019s chairside AI within SOTA\u2019s cloud-based imaging interface means that, for the first time, dentists in the United States can conveniently capture, display, and perform AI-assisted clinical evaluation of dental radiographs within a single software platform.<\/p>\n<\/div>\n

\n
<\/div>\n
\n

“Together with SOTA, we are pushing the envelope of dental imaging further toward a fully AI-powered dental future,\u201d said\u00a0Ophir Tanz<\/a>, Pearl\u2019s founder and CEO. \u201cAI will be a basic utility in dentistry and we see obvious advantages to making that utility an integral feature of the tools that dentists are already using. Through this partnership, dentists using SOTA Cloud\u00ae can seamlessly access Pearl\u2019s AI capabilities to obtain better, more consistent insights from patient imagery and present cases to patients more effectively\u2013\u2013leading to better outcomes, a higher standard of care and more productive patient communication.”<\/p>\n

Pearl\u2019s Second Opinion\u00ae is a clinical AI system cleared for use as patient-facing radiologic aid to dentists in over 90 countries. With native Second Opinion\u00ae integration, dental practices using SOTA Cloud\u00ae\u00a0 \u2013 the robust cloud-based dental imaging software which SOTA introduced last year to complement its line of state-of-the-art intraoral cameras and x-ray image sensors \u2013 can view automatic AI detections of most conditions commonly found in dental x-rays.<\/p>\n

\u201cUnderstanding the needs of clinicians and ensuring that dentists are able to focus solely on providing the best possible patient care has always been the top priority when developing our software,\u201d said SOTA Cloud founder and CEO Albert Kim. \u201cOur partnership with Pearl speaks directly to that focus by giving practices using SOTA Cloud\u00ae push-button access to the most innovative AI technology available for streamlined patient communication and improved image-based diagnostic evaluations.\u201d<\/p>\n
